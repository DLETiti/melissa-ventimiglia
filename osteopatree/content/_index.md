Osteopatree is a practice in body literacy — learning to notice, interpret, and understand your own physical structure through the literal practice of tree palpation. This isn't a metaphor or a wellness theme. Working with trees gives you a stable, external reference point for feeling how tension, movement, and structure actually behave — skills you then bring back to your own body.

This is not clinical care, and it isn't a spiritual practice. It's a teaching method: hands-on, mechanical, and direct.
